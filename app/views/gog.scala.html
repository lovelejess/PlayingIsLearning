Welcome to the 'Games of Games!'

What?

The 'Games of Games' is a card game designed to bring the ‘Playing is Learning’ initiative home with you.

It is comprised of two parts:

(1) Did You Know?

Which communicates general concepts about play.

(2) Try This At Home!

Which describes games you can play at home whenever you and your child have a few minutes.

How To Play:

When you and your child are ready to play, shuffle the card deck.

Randomly pick a card and play the game on the card for about 5 minutes.

Repeat as many times as time allows.
